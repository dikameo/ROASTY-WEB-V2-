Coffee Roasting Basics

Learn the fundamentals of roasting coffee at home. This course covers various roasting methods, bean origins, and flavor profiles.

Course Details

This course is designed for beginners who want to learn the basics of roasting coffee at home. We'll cover different roasting methods like air roasting, drum roasting, and stovetop roasting, as well as the importance of bean origins and flavor profiles. You'll get hands-on experience with each method and learn how to troubleshoot common roasting issues.
